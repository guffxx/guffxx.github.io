Voice Acting Portfolio

I have been absorbed with voice acting since I first booted up Starcraft in 1999 and listened to some of the most immersing voice acting I had heard at the time.

It has stuck with me through the years and inspired me to sit in a dark room and scream into a microphone.

My credits include many various video game characters and trailers, as well as all sorts of commercials and radio work.
